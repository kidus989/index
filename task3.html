About WWF

The World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) is a leading global organization focused on the conservation and protection of our planet's most precious ecosystems and species. Originally founded as the World Wildlife Fund in 1961, WWF is committed to making a sustainable future possible.

The Iconic Panda Symbol

WWF’s famous panda logo originated from a beloved panda named Chi Chi, who traveled from Beijing Zoo to the London Zoo the same year WWF was founded. Today, this symbol represents WWF's dedication to wildlife conservation worldwide.

Top Web Browsers

Google Chrome

Launched in 2008, Google Chrome quickly rose to fame as a fast, reliable, and intuitive web browser. With powerful customization options, Chrome is the most widely used browser in the world today.

Mozilla Firefox

Introduced by Mozilla, Firefox has built a reputation for its privacy-first approach and open-source community. It’s been a trusted choice for users worldwide since 2004.

Microsoft Edge

Replacing Internet Explorer in 2015, Microsoft Edge brings a faster and more secure browsing experience, optimized for Windows users.
